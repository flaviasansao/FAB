Our Mission

We aim to enhance the Royal Burgh of Burntisland through its horticultural, environmental and community activities, encouraging civic pride and making the town more attractive to visitors and residents. We take a ‘whole town’ approach, not just focussing on the town centre, and provide floral displays throughout the year.

Our Vision

We would like to provide the community in Burntisland with the best floral displays we can through biodiversity and environmentally friendly means.

We also aim to help everyone in our community to enjoy using the outdoor areas of our town through the use of floral displays and maintain areas of our town, such as the sea wall, car park areas, bus shelters and sheltered housing complexes.

Who we are

We are a small group of approximately 25 volunteers based in the beautiful seaside town of Burntisland who give their time for free to enhance the look of our town and promote sustainability with the community

If you feel you would like to get involved with FAB then please contact us!
